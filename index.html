About Me

I am Maithya Ndavi, a dedicated Computer Science graduate from Egerton University, born and raised in Makindu, Makueni County, Kenya. My fascination with technology sparked at St. Stephen Makindu Boys’ High School, where I cultivated a love for problem-solving and innovation, paving the way for my pursuit of a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

My academic journey has equipped me with a robust foundation in data science, machine learning, and software development. I thrive on transforming complex datasets into meaningful insights, leveraging tools like Python, SQL, and Power BI. My internship at InspireHub Limited was a defining experience, where I designed efficient database systems and crafted impactful data visualizations, fueling my passion for data-driven solutions.

A cornerstone of my academic career was my fourth-year project at Egerton University, the Industrial Attachment Management System. This web-based platform revolutionized internship coordination by offering secure user authentication, grade submission, and automated report generation. The project underscored my ability to apply technical expertise to solve real-world challenges.

With certifications in Data Science, AI, and Python from HPLife Global and Pantech Solutions, I am committed to lifelong learning and pushing the boundaries of technology. My leadership roles, including President of the Students’ Council and Chairman of the Science Club, have honed my ability to foster collaboration and drive innovation. I am eager to contribute my skills to impactful projects in data science and technology.
